August Notes
🌡️
August heat
Plan downtime from 1–4pm every day. Midday is for pools, AC, and naps — not sightseeing.
📅
Book ahead
Restaurants and beach clubs fill fast in August. Book restaurants 2–3 days ahead minimum.
🚗
Driving
Easy motorway between all three stops. Park outside the old centres — don't try to drive in.
💶
Beach clubs
Budget €15–25/day per person for sunbeds + umbrella. Worth every cent in August heat.
